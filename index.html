Circus Definition

Circus Elhelw was founded in 1889 and continues to perform its art and shows until now.It was founded by Muhammad Ali Elhelw, who tried to take advantage of the foreign shows that were being presented during the era of Khedive Ismail.He bought a small elephant from the Italian artist Hegem, who was coming to Egypt to show performance with elephants and horses, and Elhelw began training the elephant in his circus. At the beginning of the twentieth century, two lions were introduced.

Famous Animal Trainers

Muhammed Elhelw

My Grandfather Egyptian Lions coach. One of the famous Elhelw family in the world of circus, his grandfather is Muhammad Ali Elhelw,who founded the Elhelw Circus in 1889. He joined the circus school in 1962, and after graduating, he was one of the first children of the National Circus, which opened in 1966 in Agouza, Giza Governorate.

Muhammed Ali Elhelw

My Father He was nicknamed the keeper of the secrets of the pharaohs for his fame in mummification (the preservation of the body from decomposition) the first parson in the world to train the cheeta and also known the man of difficult tasks for training the fiercest and most dangerous types of African and Asian tigers and lions.

Mahmoud Elhelw

My Brother The youngest predatory animal trainer in Egypt and the Arab world, grandson of the heroic martyr Muhammad ElHelw

Ibtsam Elhelw

My Sister She is called Beauty and the Beast and plays with fire with the cubs. She is the youngest predatory animal trainer in the world, the daughter of Muhammad Ali Elhelw and the granddaughter of Muhammad Elhelw.
